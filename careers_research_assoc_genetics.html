RESEARCH ASSOCIATE: GENETICS

The candidate will perform genotyping of the candidate gene variations in association studies in childhood cancer. Additional responsibilities will include extraction of genomic DNA and RNA, PCR amplification and DNA sequencing with automated sequencer. Setting up experiments (designing genotypings), reporting results, data entry and record keeping will be part of the job. General duties will include sample and data management, quality assurance management, general lab management and preparation of data for presentations at internal and external meetings. The candidate will be expected to show a degree of independence and be good at managing your own time, experiments and data. The candidate should have an interest in genetic epidemiology. Familiarity with genetic analysis platforms and additional experience in cell culture are an advantage. This is an excellent opportunity for developing expertise in DNA-based technologies working alongside a highly active research group. The post is available from August 1 2007.

To apply for this vacancy please email your cv as a PDF file to: jobs@Humigen.org. In the subject line put your name first then the position you are applying for, thus:

            Dr Stephen Adams, SCIENTIST: MOLECULAR IMMUNOLOGY

We shall then contact you.
